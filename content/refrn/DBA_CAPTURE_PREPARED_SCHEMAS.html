4.139 DBA_CAPTURE_PREPARED_SCHEMAS

DBA_CAPTURE_PREPARED_SCHEMAS displays information about all schemas prepared for instantiation at the local database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_CAPTURE_PREPARED_SCHEMAS.
