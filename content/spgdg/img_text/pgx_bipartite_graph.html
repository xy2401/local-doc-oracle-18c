Description of the bipartite subgraph

Graph of a bipartite subgraph, explained in text before the figure. In this figure, each vertex on the left side has one or more edges connecting it to one or more vertices on the right side, and one vertex on the right side has an edge connecting to a vertex on the left side. No vertices have edge connections to any vertex on the same side.
