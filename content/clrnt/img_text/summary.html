Description of the illustration summary.gif

This screen shot shows the Summary window.

The summary window states that the information needed for the wizard has been specified.

There is a summary that includes the entire assembly path to CLRLibary1.dll and the Project Name: CLRLibrary1.

The screen shot states that the CLRLibrary1.dll will be copied to the $Oracle_ HOME\bin\clr directory of ORACLE and 1 stored procedure will be deployed to ORACLE.

There is a Show Script button, as well as Back, Next, Finish, and Cancel buttons.
