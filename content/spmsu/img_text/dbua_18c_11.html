The Upgrade Results window shows the database name, release, and Oracle home location for the source and target databases.

This screen shows the upgrade starting for the CDB root database instance. Preupgrade steps are completed successfully. The successful completion is indicated by checkmarks . Steps in progress are indicated by a watch face icon.

Underneath the Upgrade Results table, there are two buttons on the left side: Alert Log, and Progress Log. On the right side are three buttons Retry, which you can click to retry a failed check. Ignore, which you can click to override a precheck error and continue the upgrade. Pause enables you pause the upgrade.

Underneath the buttons, there is a text field. to display additional information about issues.

At the bottom of the window are Help, Back, Next, Finish, and Close buttons.
